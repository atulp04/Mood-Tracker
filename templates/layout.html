How to Use Mood Tracker
Track Your Mood

Select a mood icon that best represents how you feel today. Add some notes about what influenced your mood.

View Your History

Check your mood history in the calendar view or through the trend charts to identify patterns.

Get Insights

After tracking for 7 days, you'll receive personalized insights and suggestions to help improve your emotional well-being.

Export Your Data

Export your mood data in CSV or JSON format for backup or analysis using the Export Data button.

Privacy

All your data is stored locally on your device. We respect your privacy - no data is sent to any servers.
